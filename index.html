about

us

A challenge that will take place in Los Santos area in its first edition this 2023, with three main stages, this competition is designed for the greatest exponents of national and international cycling in different categories.

5 days of great physical and mental demand, will start in San Marcos de Tarrazú, will cross the main districts of the area and will culminate back in the main district.
